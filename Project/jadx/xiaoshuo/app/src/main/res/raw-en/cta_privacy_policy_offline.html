Introduction

Duokan is a platform provided by Beijing Duokan Technology Co., Ltd. and its affiliated companies (hereinafter referred to as "Xiaomi", "we", "our", or "us") that provides you with online and local books, downloads, and audiobooks.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy sets out the principles on which Duokan operates, and constitutes an important part of Xiaomi Privacy Policy. In the event of inconsistency between this Privacy Policy and Xiaomi Privacy Policy with respect to Duokan, the former shall prevail. For the terms and conditions not stipulated in this Privacy Policy, Xiaomi Privacy Policy shall prevail. Terms and conditions regarding the protection of minors, security measures, and cross-border data transmission can be found in Xiaomi Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to your access and use of our products and services through the Duokan app, Duokan official website, Duokan mobile website, Duokan e-reader devices, and Duokan software development kit (SDK) for third-party websites and applications. This Privacy Policy is designed with your needs in mind, and it is important that you have a comprehensive understanding of our personal information collection and usage practices, while ensuring that ultimately, you have control of your personal information provided to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, process, and store any information that you give us when you use Duokan. Under this Privacy Policy, "personal information" means information that can be used to directly or indirectly identify an individual, either from that information alone or from that information combined with other information about that individual available to us. We will use your personal information strictly following this Privacy Policy.

Ultimately, what we want is the best for all our users. Should you have any questions about our data handling practices as summarized in this Privacy Policy, please contact us via https://privacy.mi.com/support to address your specific concerns. We will be happy to hear from you.

If you agree to this Privacy Policy, it only means that you agree to the collection of personal information when using basic features. It does not mean that you agree to the collection of personal information when using additional features. The personal information required to use additional features will be collected with your consent when you use them.

1. What information we collect and how we use it

1.1 Personal information that we collect and use with your authorization

The purpose of collecting personal information is to provide you with products and/or services, and to ensure that we comply with applicable laws, regulations, and other regulatory requirements. You have the right to choose whether or not to provide the information we have requested, but in most cases, if you do not provide your personal information, we may not be able to provide you with our services. The related features include:

1.1.1 Account sign-in

You can use your Xiaomi Account to sign in to Duokan with one tap. To provide this service, we will collect your Xiaomi Account ID, nickname, and profile photo. Signing in to Duokan with your Xiaomi Account allows you to access more comprehensive services. When signing in, you can enter information (including your gender and reading preferences) that we use to recommend books to you. If you choose not to create an account and sign in, you can still use Duokan's basic services, including browsing and searching.

You can also use WeChat to create or sign in to a Duokan account. If you do so, we'll collect your nickname and profile photo.

1.1.2 Searching, reading, downloading, and adding books to your bookshelf

You can use Duokan to quickly find and read books and other media, including novels, magazines, and comics. You can also add the books you are interested in to your bookshelf or download them to your device. Duokan can also record your bookmarks, excerpts, notes, and reading progress.

In order to provide these services, we may collect your system version information, search terms, language settings, app version, and accelerometer data (only used to recognize and adapt to whether your device is in landscape or portrait mode). When performing copyright verification for online books, we may also collect your device identifier (OAID) for Android Q and later. We use the aforementioned personal information to improve and optimize our services. This information is associated with your account.

You can also search for specific content or services. We store the keywords and phrases you've entered so that you can perform the same search without re-entering them. Please note that your search history cannot be used to identify you individually, and so is not considered part of your personal information. We therefore have the right to use it for any purpose. Only when your search history is used alongside other information and thus can be used to identify you will we regard your search history as personal information. Only then will it be processed and protected according to this Privacy Policy.

1.1.3 Audiobook features

You can download iFLYTEK voice services to use audiobook features. In order to provide this service, we will collect device identifiers (OAID (for Android Q and later), UUID (only collected on iOS devices)), system version information, user behavior data (download, purchase, and use of voice packs). Xiaomi eReader devices don't support this feature.

1.1.4 Purchasing items

You can earn points and coins to purchase paid books by completing check-in tasks, participating in activities, topping up your account, watching ads, tapping on ads, and via daily gifts for VIP members. When you make purchases and payments, we may collect your device identifier (OAID for Android Q and later), Android ID, UUID (only for iOS devices), accelerometer data (for shake ads and dynamic ads), Xiaomi Account ID, and points, coins, and order records in order to deliver the books you purchase. After you place an order, you can choose payment services provided by Duokan’s affiliates or our third-party payment cooperators (including Mi Pay, WeChat Pay, Alipay, and other payment channels, hereinafter referred to as “payment processors”). We do not collect your personal information when you make a payment, however, we provide payment processors with your order records for them to be able to complete the payment.

Points, coins, and order history

This information is considered to be sensitive personal information. If you refuse to provide it, you won't be able to purchase paid books. Please rest assured that we will adopt industry-standard practices and take all efforts to protect the security of your sensitive personal information. If you choose not to provide this type of information, you may not be able to use certain Duokan features normally, but this will not affect your use of other features of the service.

1.1.5 Interactions

You can choose to share information about books you've read and your opinions on your Duokan home page. To this end, we may collect your account ID, device ID (OAID, for Android Q and later), reading dates and durations, and names of books you've read to display this information to you and people you associate with on the app. We will also collect your reviews and opinions that you post on your profile.

You can choose to follow people you're interested in. To provide this feature, we will collect who you follow and who follows you and display this information to you.

When displaying your updates, we will also collect information about your reposts, comments, and likes of book reviews.

1.2 Personal information you may choose whether to authorize us to collect and use

1.2.1 To improve your experience using Duokan, we may collect and use your personal information for the additional features below to work. If you choose not to provide this personal information, you can still use Duokan, but you may not have access to these additional features. The additional features include:

1.2.1.1 Additional features based on access to the camera and photos (only for iOS devices) and permission to upload images: This permission is used to sign in via scanning a QR code and to upload profile photos and wallpapers (only for Xiaomi eReader devices).

1.2.1.2 Additional features based on Bluetooth permissions: This permission is used to detect the connection status of your Bluetooth earphones when using audiobook features, and stop playback automatically when earphones are disconnected (Xiaomi eReader devices don't support this feature).

1.2.1.3 Additional features based on access to device storage: This permission is used to import files from your device to allow you to read them in the app (only applicable to Android 11 and earlier).

1.2.1.4 Additional features based on permission to record audio: This permission is used to provide search features for voice search (only available for Xiaomi eReader devices).

1.2.1.5 Additional features based on access to Calendar: This permission is used to provide event reminders and check-ins (only applicable to Android devices).

1.2.1.6 Additional features based on permission to add items to external storage: This permission is used to provide audiobook features (only applicable to Android devices).

1.2.1.7 Additional features based on permission to access external storage: This permission is used to import books, provide audiobook features, and upload images when submitting feedback (only applicable to Android devices).

1.2.1.8 Additional features based on permission to access accounts stored on the device: This permission is used for one-tap sign-in features (only applicable to Android 8.0 and earlier).

1.2.1.9 Additional features based on location access: This permission is used to access the network name when your device is connected to the network (only applicable to 7" Xiaomi eReader devices).

When you use the above additional features, you may need to grant permission for us to access your camera, photos (only on iOS devices), and Gallery (albums), add items to the calendar, access and add items to device storage, access device information, record audio (only for Xiaomi eReader devices), and access location (only for 7" Xiaomi eReader devices) to enable the collection and use of the information required for these features. Please note that if you grant these permissions, it means you consent to our collection and use of this personal information. However, if you rescind permission, you will withdraw your consent and we will cease to collect or use your personal information, in which case we will no longer be able to provide you with these features. Your withdrawal of consent will not affect the processing of your personal information conducted based on your consent up until the point of withdrawal.

In addition, as regards 1.2.1.1 and 1.2.1.2, when you're using Android P or earlier, we will request permission to access call status when performing statistics and service push notifications.

1.2.2 When your device is connected to the network, we may collect some network-related information. This includes the mode of the device's currently connected WLAN network (LAN mode, wireless mode), the device's assigned IP address, the name of the connected WLAN, WLAN signal strength, and the received WLAN signal strength indicator (i.e., device RSSI). It is used to display the device's network connection status and assist in troubleshooting and locating problems. This permission is only applicable to 7" Xiaomi eReader devices.

1.2.3 When we recommend and display personalized content to you, we will collect relevant data and use algorithms to show you information about books or services that may interest you, or show you books you might like when you use search features. We may also collect device identifiers (OAID for Android Q and later), Android ID, search keywords, reading history (including reading time, book consumption behavior (views, clicks, read books, downloads, books added to and removed from bookshelf, and audiobook consumption)), user feedback, your gender, and device model (Android). We may combine data we collect across services and from your different devices and link it in order to deliver a consistent cross-device experience. We may combine information from one service with information from other services when providing you with services, personalized content, and recommendations. You may opt out at any time by turning off recommendations in Settings. For detailed information, refer to "Your rights" below.

We will not make cross-platform ad recommendations to you based on your personal information.

We may also collect other types of information which are not directly or indirectly linked to an individual and which may not be defined as personal information according to applicable local laws.
